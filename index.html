About Us

Code the Change at Berkeley is an organization that empowers computer scientists and engineers to use their technical skills for social good through non-profit projects.

We form project teams to assist non-profits in achieving their goals, whether it be through web development or mobile app design. Our project teams are comprised of Cal students with a passion for both technology and the social sector. We believe that as long as you’re willing to learn, you’re welcome to join us in our project teams where you can develop your technical abilities and form meaningful relationships with members who share similar interests.

Code the Change has active chapters on both the UC Berkeley and Stanford campuses. You can learn more about the Stanford chapter here.
